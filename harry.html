About Us

Maxcfrey Consultants is known for its innovative designs and high-quality architectural services. Our team is led by the well-trained architect Harrison Owuor, who brings a wealth of experience and expertise to every project. Based in Sudan and Kenya, we are dedicated to creating structures that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing.

Our team at Maxcfrey Consultants is passionate about delivering exceptional architecture that enhances the lives of the people who live, work, and play in our spaces. We combine creativity with practicality to design buildings that are not only visually stunning but also sustainable and efficient. Whether you're looking to build a new home, redesign a commercial space, or embark on an urban planning project, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

Harrison Owuor, our lead architect, has over 20 years of experience in the field. His innovative approach and attention to detail have earned him a reputation as one of the top architects in the region. He is committed to working closely with clients to ensure that every project meets their needs and exceeds their expectations.
